Eiichiro Oda: One Piece Creator

About Eiichiro Oda

  • In 1992, the seventeen-year-old Oda created his first full-length manga titled Wanted. He then submitted this as an entry to
    Shueisha's Tezuka Awards, a semi-annual event sponsored by Shueisha with the event of cultivating young manga artists.
  • Wanted! ( 1992) God's Present for the Future (神から未来のプレゼント, Kami Kara Mirai no Purezento, 1993) Ikki Yakō
    (一鬼夜行, 1993) Monsters (1994) Romance Dawn (first version, 1996) Romance Dawn (second version, 1996) One Piece (1997 – ongoing)
  • On November 7, 2004, 29-years-old Eiichiro Oda married then 26-years-old Chiaki Inaba (稲葉ちあき Inaba Chiaki) (Kanagawa prefecture, Kantō,
    Japan, January 4, 1978), a former model, actress and "racequeen" in Japan's automobile racing sport since 1992 to early 2000s.
